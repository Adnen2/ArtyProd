We Create
Awesome Work

The agency, "ArtyProd", is a company that specializes in graphic design, audiovisual production, and 3D design. They aim to create a web application that showcases their various services to the public.

The application allows users to register through a form, which requires personal information such as their name, email, and phone number. This information is used to personalize their experience and to enable the agency to contact them if necessary.

Visitors who do not wish to register are able to browse the agency's completed projects to gain an understanding of their services. However, only registered users are able to access the agency's services. Without registering, users will not have any additional privileges beyond what a simple visitor has.
